## Business Recommendations

### 1. Enhance Customer Satisfaction & After-Sales Support
- Steady decline in customer satisfaction/feedback ratings indicates growing dissatisfaction.
- Invest in stronger post-purchase follow-ups, proactive communication, and a robust customer support program.
- Expected impact: Improved customer trust, increased satisfaction ratings, and potential attraction of new customers.

### 2. Optimize Shipping & Fulfillment Processes
- Shipping delays increased significantly, contributing to negative reviews.
- Recommended actions: Partner with reliable shipping providers, improve warehouse logistics, and implement shipment tracking for customers.
- Expected impact: Faster delivery, better customer experience, and improved satisfaction ratings.

### 3. Reassess Pricing and Discount Strategies
- Average discount across credit cards remains below 1%, indicating underutilized promotion opportunities.
- Recommended actions: Introduce targeted and seasonal discounts to boost short-term revenue and enhance competitive positioning.
- Expected impact: Increased sales and better customer engagement.

### 4. Focus Marketing Efforts on High-Performing Regions and Brands
- Texas and California lead in total customers; Chevrolet and Ford dominate vehicle sales.
- Recommended actions: Increase marketing efforts in these high-performing states and brands, while implementing strategies in underperforming regions.
- Expected impact: Improved marketing ROI and stronger market penetration.

### 5. Address Decline in Order Volume and Retention
- Orders and revenue decreased by ~35%, indicating retention challenges.
- Recommended actions: Launch loyalty programs, referral incentives, and targeted email campaigns to engage existing customers and encourage repeat purchases.
- Expected impact: Increased customer retention, higher engagement, and improved revenue.
